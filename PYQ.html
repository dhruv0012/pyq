Some important identities -

Cos^2Q + sin^2Q = 1

  • Sec^2Q - tan^2Q = 1
  • Cosec^2Q - Cot^2Q = 1

    • Relation of 6 trignometric ratios with respect to perpendicular (P), hypotenuse (H) and base (B) -

    • Sin Q = P/H
    • Cos Q = B/H
    • Tan Q = P/B
    • Cosec Q = H/P
    • Sec Q = H/B
    • Cot Q = B/P
